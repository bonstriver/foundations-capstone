Singer-songwriter John Mayer has been on the music scene for over two decades now. Even if you haven't heard of the seven-time Grammy winner, you have still probably heard his music on your mom's "Now That's What I Call Music!" CD from the backseat of the Dodge Grand Caravan in 2008. While his legacy may be cemented as the womanizing pop-blues balladeer from the early 2000's, did you know...John Mayer shreds, too?
